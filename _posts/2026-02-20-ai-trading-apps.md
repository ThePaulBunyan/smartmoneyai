Want to start investing but don't know where to begin? These AI-powered apps make trading easy.

## Top AI Trading Apps

### 1. Robinhood
- **AI Features:** Fractional shares, cash management
- **Best for:** Beginners
- **Cost:** Free

### 2. Webull
- **AI Features:** Technical analysis tools, paper trading
- **Best for:** Active traders
- **Cost:** Free

### 3. M1 Finance
- **AI Features:** Automated portfolio management
- **Best for:** Passive investors
- **Cost:** Free

### 4. Betterment
- **AI Features:** Robo-advisor, automatic rebalancing
- **Best for:** Hands-off investors
- **Cost:** 0.25% annually

### 5. Acorns
- **AI Features:** Round-up investing, portfolio selection
- **Best for:** Micro-investors
- **Cost:** $3-5/month

## How AI Helps Beginners

1. **Automated investing** - Set it and forget it
2. **Risk assessment** - AI evaluates your comfort level
3. **Portfolio rebalancing** - Keeps your allocation on track
4. **Tax optimization** - Minimizes your tax burden

## Which Should You Choose?

| Your Goal | Best App |
|-----------|----------|
| Learn to trade | Webull |
| Passive investing | Betterment |
| Micro-investing | Acorns |
| Simple interface | Robinhood |

## Risk Warning

All investing involves risk. AI tools help but don't guarantee profits. Start with money you can afford to lose.

**Start small, learn often.**
