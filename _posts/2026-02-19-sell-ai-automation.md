# How to Sell AI Automation to Local Businesses

Every business needs automation. Here's how to sell it to them.

## Who to Target

### Best Customers
1. **Real estate agents** - High volume, repetitive tasks
2. **Insurance agencies** - Lots of paperwork
3. **Law firms** - Document-heavy
4. **Medical offices** - Scheduling, billing
5. **Restaurants** - Orders, reservations

### What They Care About
- Saving time
- Reducing errors
- Better customer service
- More revenue

## How to Find Them

### 1. Cold Outreach
- Email them directly
- Call and offer free audit
- LinkedIn messages

### 2. Content Marketing
- Post about their problems
- Show solutions
- Build trust

### 3. Referrals
- Ask for introductions
- Partner with complementary businesses

## What to Offer

### Package 1: Quick Win
- One specific automation
- $500-1,000
- 1-week setup

### Package 2: Full Automation
- Complete workflow
- $2,000-5,000
- 2-4 week setup

### Package 3: Ongoing
- Full management
- $300-1,000/month
- Continuous improvement

## The Sales Process

1. **Free audit** - Show what's wasting their time
2. **Proposal** - Clear solution with ROI
3. **Demo** - Show it working
4. **Close** - Sign and implement

## What to Say

"I help [industry] businesses save 20+ hours/week using AI automation. Want me to show you how?"

Simple. Direct. Effective.

## The Market

- 30+ million small businesses in US
- Most need help with automation
- Most don't know how to do it

**This is a $10B+ opportunity.**
