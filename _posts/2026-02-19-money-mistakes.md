# 5 Money Mistakes Rich People Never Make

The wealthy think differently about money. Here's what they avoid:

## 1. Buying Things That Depreciate
❌ New cars
❌ Designer clothes
❌ Electronics

Rich people buy assets that appreciate:
✅ Real estate
✅ Stocks
✅ Businesses

## 2. Saving Instead of Investing
❌ Putting money in savings (loses to inflation)
✅ Investing in index funds
✅ Investing in yourself

## 3. Working for Money
❌ Trading time for dollars
✅ Building assets
✅ Creating passive income

## 4. Having No Financial Plan
❌ "I'll figure it out"
✅ Clear goals
✅ Written plan
✅ Monthly reviews

## 5. Following the Crowd
❌ Buying what's popular
✅ Thinking independently
✅ Doing own research

## The Fix

1. Track every expense
2. Invest 20% of income minimum
3. Read 10 pages about money daily
4. Find mentors
5. Take calculated risks

**Your wealth is your responsibility.**

Start today. Small actions compound.
