# Best AI Side Hustles in 2026

AI opens up new income streams. Here are the best side hustles you can start today.

## High-Potential AI Side Hustles

### 1. AI Content Writing
- Write blog posts, emails, ads with AI assistance
- Platforms: Upwork, Fiverr, Contently
- Earn: $500-5,000/month
- Start: Now

### 2. AI Video Editing
- Use AI tools to edit videos faster
- Create YouTube content for clients
- Earn: $1,000-3,000/month
- Start: Learn CapCut, Descript

### 3. AI Prompt Engineering
- Write prompts for businesses
- Sell prompt templates
- Earn: $200-2,000/month
- Start: Create a prompt library

### 4. AI Course Creation
- Teach others what you know about AI
- Sell on Udemy, Skillshare, Gumroad
- Earn: $100-10,000/month
- Start: Outline your first course

### 5. AI Consulting
- Help businesses use AI tools
- Charge $50-200/hour
- Earn: $2,000-10,000/month
- Start: Offer free consultations

## How to Start

1. **Pick one** — Don't try everything at once
2. **Build a portfolio** — Do free work initially
3. **Raise rates** — As you gain experience
4. **Scale** — Outsource or create products

## Our Recommendation

Start with **content writing** or **prompt engineering** — low barrier to entry, quick to monetize.

**The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is now.**
