# How to Use AI to Make Money in 2026

AI isn't just for techies. Here's how anyone can earn with AI.

## Proven Money-Making Methods

### 1. Sell AI-Generated Art
- Use Midjourney, DALL-E to create designs
- Sell on Etsy, Redbubble, Printful
- Products: T-shirts, mugs, prints, stickers

### 2. Build AI Websites
- Use AI website builders (10Web, Durable)
- Create sites for local businesses
- Charge $500-2,000 per site

### 3. AI-Powered Services
- Social media management (AI scheduling)
- Email marketing (AI writing)
- Data entry (AI processing)

### 4. Create AI Templates
- Build Notion, Excel, ChatGPT templates
- Sell on Gumroad, Etsy
- Price: $10-100 each

### 5. YouTube Automation
- Use AI to write, edit, voiceover
- Grow faceless channels
- Monetize at 1K subscribers

## Step-by-Step Start

**Week 1:** Pick one method, learn the tool
**Week 2:** Create your first product/service
**Week 3:** Get first customer (even free)
**Week 4:** Scale and optimize

## Keys to Success

- Start before you're ready
- Charge for your work early
- Reinvest profits
- Build in public

**You don't need skills — you need action.**
