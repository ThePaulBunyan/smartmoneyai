# How to Use AI to Find Dividend Stocks

Passive income from dividends — AI makes finding them easier.

## What Are Dividend Stocks?

Companies that pay you regularly — just for owning shares. Like getting paid to be an investor.

## How AI Helps

1. **Screening** — Find stocks matching your criteria instantly
2. **Analysis** — Evaluate payout sustainability
3. **Comparison** — Compare dividend yields side-by-side
4. **Prediction** — Assess if dividends will grow

## Best Tools for Dividend Investing

### 1. Dividend.com
- Comprehensive screening
- Dividend aristocrats list
- Yield analysis

### 2. Seeking Alpha
- Dividend safety ratings
- Earnings analysis
- Community insights

### 3. Stockanalysis.com
- Free dividend screener
- Payout history
- Growth trends

## ChatGPT for Dividends

Ask ChatGPT:
- "What are safe dividend stocks for retirement?"
- "Compare Apple's dividend to Microsoft's"
- "What is a good dividend yield in 2026?"

## What to Look For

| Metric | Good | Warning |
|--------|------|---------|
| Yield | 3-5% | Over 8% |
| Payout Ratio | Under 60% | Over 80% |
| Growth | 5%+ annual | Declining |

## Our Strategy

1. Use screener to find candidates
2. Ask AI to analyze fundamentals
3. Check payout sustainability
4. Diversify across sectors

**Building dividend income takes time — but AI speeds up the research.**

## Start Here

- Open a brokerage (Fidelity, Schwab, or Vanguard)
- Use a free screener to find 10 candidates
- Research each with AI
- Start with small positions

**Consistency beats timing — start investing today.**
