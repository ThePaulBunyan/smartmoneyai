# How to Automate Your Budget with AI in 2026

Manual budgeting is tedious. AI can do most of the work for you — automatically.

## Why Automate Your Budget?

- Saves 30+ minutes per week
- Catches spending you miss
- Finds subscription savings
- Provides real-time insights

## Best AI Budgeting Tools

### 1. Rocket Money (formerly Truebill)
- Automatically finds subscriptions
- Negotiates bills for you
- Free to use (takes % of savings)

### 2. Copilot
- AI-powered expense tracking
- Real-time categorizations
- Works with all US banks

### 3. YNAB (With AI Features)
- Proactive suggestions
- Debt payoff planning
- Goal tracking

## How to Set It Up

1. **Connect your accounts** — Link bank accounts to your chosen tool
2. **Let AI categorize** — Review initial categorizations
3. **Set spending rules** — Create custom rules for recurring expenses
4. **Check weekly** — Review AI insights once a week

## Pro Tips

- Review AI categorizations monthly
- Set savings goals in the app
- Use bill negotiation features

**Start automating today — your future self will thank you.**
