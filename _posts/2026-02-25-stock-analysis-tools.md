# AI Tools for Stock Analysis: Complete Guide

Stop guessing. Let AI help you pick better stocks.

## Types of Stock Analysis

### Fundamental Analysis
Evaluating company health: revenue, earnings, debt

### Technical Analysis
Reading charts and patterns

### Sentiment Analysis
Measuring market mood

## Best AI Tools by Category

### For Fundamental Analysis

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| Yahoo Finance | Free data | Free |
| Finviz | Screening | Free/$39 |
| Seeking Alpha | Research | Free |

### For Technical Analysis

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| TradingView | Charts | Free/$20 |
| TrendSpider | Automation | Free/$99 |
| StockCharts | Pro tools | Free/$20 |

### For Sentiment Analysis

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|------|----------|------|
| StockTwits | Social sentiment | Free |
| FinGPT | AI analysis | Coming soon |

## How to Use AI for Stock Picks

1. **Start with fundamentals** — Use Finviz to find healthy companies
2. **Check the numbers** — Ask ChatGPT to explain financials
3. **Look at charts** — Use TradingView for patterns
4. **Check sentiment** — What is the market saying?

## Red Flags

- ❌ AI "guarantees" returns
- ❌ Signals without explanation
- ❌ Unrealistic backtests

## Our Recommendation

Start with **Yahoo Finance + ChatGPT** — free and powerful enough for most investors.

**Always do your own research before investing.**
