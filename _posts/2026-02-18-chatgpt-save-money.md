# How to Use ChatGPT to Save Money Every Month

ChatGPT isn't just for answering questions — it can help you save real money. Here's how.

## 1. Create a Custom Budget

Ask ChatGPT: *"Create a monthly budget for someone earning $4,000/month who wants to save 20%"*

You'll get a customized breakdown instantly.

## 2. Analyze Your Spending

Paste your bank statement (remove sensitive info) and ask:
- "What patterns do you see in my spending?"
- "Where can I cut costs?"

## 3. Negotiate Bills

Ask ChatGPT to write negotiation scripts for:
- Internet provider
- Insurance premiums
- Subscription services

## 4. Meal Planning for Savings

Prompt: *"Create a cheap weekly meal plan for $50 that includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner"*

## 5. Side Hustle Ideas

Ask: *"What low-cost side hustles can I start with less than $100?"*

## Pro Tips

- Be specific with prompts
- Iterate on the answers
- Use it for research, not as financial advice

**Start using ChatGPT for money matters today — it's free and surprisingly effective.**
